About Me

Diego Bravo is a PhD student of Electrical Engineering at the Universidad Nacional de Colombia-UNAL, where he works at Computer Imaging and Medical Applications Laboratory (CIM@LAB). He earned a Mechatronics Engineer degree from the Universidad Militar in 2014 and a MSc in Biomedical Engineer degree from the UNAL in 2020. His research work revolves around machine learning, information retrieval, and computer vision, with a particular focus on the representation, indexing, and automatic analysis of multimodal data (data encompassing different types of information: textual, visual, signals, etc.).
